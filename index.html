Yunhao Yang is a Ph.D. student in computer science at The University of Texas at Austin. He started his Ph.D. program in Fall 2022. Prior to this, he received B.S. and M.S. degrees in computer science, and a B.A. in mathematics from UT Austin. His current research interests include connecting large language models to formal methods, and privacy in machine learning models.
