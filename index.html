About Me

I'm a student at the University of Texas at Austin, major in computer science and mathematics. I'm currently working in the Department of Information, Risk, and Operations Management, McCombs School of Business as an Undergraduate Research Assistant and doing research in deep learning with Professor Andrew Whinston.
My research concentration is Machine Learning, specifically in Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision. I have participated in various research projects in the area of NLP and deep learning. Currently, I'm enrolling in a computer vision project under the supervision of Professor Chandrajit Bajaj. I'm sufficient on machine learning frameworks such as Tensorflow, Keras, Scikit Learn and PyTorch.
