About Me

I'm a third-year college student at the University of Texas at Austin, major in computer science and mathematics. I'm currently working in the Department of Information, Risk, and Operations Management, McCombs School of Business as an Undergraduate Research Assistant and doing research in Natural Language Processing.

My research concentration is Machine Learning, specifically in Natural Language Processing and Data Mining. I have participated in various research projects in the area of NLP and deep learning. I'm sufficient on machine learning frameworks such as Pytorch, Scikit Learn and Tensorflow. See Research

My interests are Data Analysis and Machine Learning, Software Design and Mobile App Development.
