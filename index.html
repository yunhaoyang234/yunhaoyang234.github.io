About Me

I'm a second-year master's student at the University of Texas at Austin, major in computer science. I'm currently working in the Department of Information, Risk, and Operations Management, McCombs School of Business as an Undergraduate Research Assistant and doing research in deep learning with Professor Andrew Whinston.
My research concentration is Machine Learning, specifically in Differential Privacy, Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision. I have participated in various research projects in the area of deep learning. I'm currently working with Ufuk Topcu on the privacy aspect of machine learning. Previously, I worked in the CVC Group with Professor Chandrajit Bajaj. I'm sufficient on machine learning frameworks such as Tensorflow, Keras, Scikit Learn and PyTorch.
