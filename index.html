Yunhao Yang is a 4th-year Ph.D. student in computer science at The University of Texas at Austin. Prior to this, he received B.S. and M.S. degrees in computer science, and a B.A. in mathematics from UT Austin. He is currently a member of the Center for Autonomy, supervised by Prof. Ufuk Topcu. His research interests include integrating large language models and formal methods for autonomy and uncertainty quantification in AI.

In addition, he received the Amazon Science Research Fellowship (by the UT-Amazon Hub) in the 2024~2025 academic year and interned at General Motors (GM) in the summer of 2024 and 2026, conducting research on autonomous driving.
